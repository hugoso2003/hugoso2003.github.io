Kansai Region at a Glance

The Kansai region, the historical and cultural heart of Japan, offers a compelling blend of ancient tradition and vibrant modernity. Home to former imperial capitals like Kyoto and Nara, it's the birthplace of geisha culture, the tea ceremony, and samurai ethos. Today, it stands as a major economic hub, with Osaka's dynamic food scene and Kobe's international port. This guide focuses on the prime travel season of mid-October to mid-November, when the climate is mild and the autumn foliage is spectacular.
